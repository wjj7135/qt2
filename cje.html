Chief Executive Officer

Changsoo Je, PhD

Changsoo Je is the Chief Executive Officer of Qtairo Inc. He previously served as a Research Professor at Sogang University and held the roles of CTO, Executive Director, and Director of Research Center at Grand Power Solution Co., Ltd. (2024). He also served as CTO and Director of Research Center at e2on Inc. (2023-2024). He holds a Ph.D. in Media Technology from Sogang University, with a background in computer vision and 3D range imaging.
